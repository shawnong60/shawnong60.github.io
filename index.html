About Me

I am final-year Ph.D. candidate at the Center for Applied Mathematics at Cornell University, where I have the pleasure of being advised by Dexter Kozen. My interests lie in Theory of Computation and Mathematical Logic. Prior to starting at Cornell, I received my Masters in Mathematics from the University of Pennsylvania. Before that, I received my Bachelors in Mathematics, Computer Science, and Logic, also from the University of Pennsylvania, where I graduated summa cum laude. Outside of research, I am passionate about teaching and have received teaching awards from Computer Science departments of both Cornell (2022) and Penn (2018).

I am currently on the academic job market for the 2025-2026 academic year.
