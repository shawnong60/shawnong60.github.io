Here are some of the things you can do to ensure you are prepared for class each day:

  • Complete the assigned reading, and associated tasks. These will be posted to Gradescope (and announced on Piazza if I remember).
  • Bring any questions you have from the reading — there will be time during class to address these.
  • Arrive punctually, and ready to participate. Much of our in-class work will be completed in pairs or small groups, so you have the chance to learn from your peers.
  • Don't be afraid to be wrong! Math is very hard to learn, especially if you find yourself trying to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Don't be afraid to ask questions! If you are confused, chances are that others in the class are too.
